At its core, Autonomous Systems boil down to a very finite set of concepts that if mastered, allow one to truly build something fucking magical.

  • Data Strutures
  • Algorithms
  • Computer Architecture
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Modeling of Dynamic Systems
  • Control Theory (Specifically Linear & Optimal)
  • Linear Algebra
  • Optimization Theory
  • Game Theory
  • Computational Methods

These topics, from what I have gathered in my admittingly small amount of time of being in this field, form the basis of >99% of Autonomy Systems. On the software side most any tool, library, or service you come across are merely an abstractions of one of the above topics that relieve a developer of some responsibility, headache, or redundancy.
